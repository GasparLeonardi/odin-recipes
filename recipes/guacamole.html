Guacamole

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico.

It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Slice three ripe avocados in half, remove the pit and scoop them into a mixing bowl.
  2. Then use a fork to gently mash them to your desired level of chunky or smooth.
  3. Add the onions, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, garlic, lime juice and salt and stir everything together.
